Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

FormAlign (“we,” “us”) operates the website formalign.fit. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It is written for the site as it exists today: a pre-launch informational site with an early-access email list.

What we collect

  • Early-access signups: your name, your email address (or mobile number), and your answers to a few short questions about what you’re looking for and how you found us. Submissions are processed and stored by our form provider, Formspree, on our behalf.
  • Emails you send us: if you contact hello@formalign.fit or press@formalign.fit, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.

What we use it for

  • Sending occasional FormAlign progress updates and early-access information.
  • Understanding aggregate interest (e.g. how many signups want correction vs. tracking).
  • Responding when you contact us.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties except the service providers needed to operate the list (e.g. our form/email provider).

Cookies and analytics

This site does not set tracking cookies. If we add analytics, we intend to use a privacy-focused, cookieless tool and will update this policy accordingly.

Your choices

  • Every update email includes a way to unsubscribe, and unsubscribing is immediate.
  • You can ask us to delete your email from the list at any time by writing to hello@formalign.fit.

Data retention

We keep early-access emails until you unsubscribe or ask for deletion, or until the list is retired.

Changes

If this policy changes materially (for example, when we launch the companion app or e-commerce), we will update this page and the “last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@formalign.fit.
