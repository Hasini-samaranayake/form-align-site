How it works

Form isn’t a feeling. It’s a measurement.

A trainer judges your form by watching where your weight goes. FormAlign measures the same thing directly — through the mat itself.

Sensing

The mat feels where your weight actually is.

Pressure sensors are embedded across multiple zones of the mat, reading how your weight is distributed — front to back and left to right — across every point of contact: feet, hands, knees, hips.

At the start of each session the mat calibrates to you, so readings reflect your body and setup rather than a factory assumption. From there, it samples continuously — fast enough to catch a shift as it happens, not after.

Feedback

Correction that arrives within the rep.

When your distribution drifts outside the target range for the exercise you’re doing, the mat cues you immediately — a visual and audio signal on the mat itself, no phone required, plus richer guidance in the companion app.

This isn’t a concept: the correction loop runs on the working prototype today. Shift your weight and it responds in a fraction of a second — fast enough to fix the rep you’re in, not the one after it.

Exercise targets

Every exercise has its own “right place.”

A lunge loads you differently than a plank; a bridge differently than a quadruped. FormAlign compares your live distribution against a target profile for the specific exercise you’re doing, selected in the app — so “good form” means good form for this movement, not a generic average.

The target library is being built and validated with certified pilates instructors, exercise by exercise.

The intelligence roadmap

Rules today. Learning tomorrow.

We’re building the intelligence in deliberate stages — each one earns the next.

1

Expert-defined targets Working now

Precise, instructor-validated target ranges per exercise, checked against your live readings in real time. Simple, transparent, and already running on hardware.

2

Exercise recognition Next

The mat learns to recognize which exercise you’re doing from the pressure pattern itself — no manual selection, just flow through your session.

3

Personalized coaching The goal

As the mat learns your history, targets adapt to your body, your progress, and your goals — form feedback that gets more personal the longer you practice.
