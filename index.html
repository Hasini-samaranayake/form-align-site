The problem

Practicing alone means practicing unchecked.

In a lunge, a plank, or a bridge, where your weight sits determines whether the exercise builds you up or quietly wears you down. Poor weight distribution reduces effectiveness and drives overuse injury — and at home, there’s no instructor to catch it before it becomes a habit.

Whether you want a correction cue in the moment or just the data over time, FormAlign keeps track.

How it works

A trainer’s eye, translated into pressure.

1

Step on the mat

Embedded pressure sensors read how your weight meets the mat — feet, hands, knees.

2

It learns your baseline

A quick calibration tunes the mat to you and your setup at the start of each session.

3

It reads every shift

As you move, the mat tracks your weight distribution against the target for that exercise.

4

It cues you instantly

Drift off target and the mat tells you — gentle, immediate, and within the rep.
