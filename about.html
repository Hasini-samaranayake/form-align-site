About

Expert form feedback shouldn’t require an expert in the room.

In a studio, an instructor can correct a shift in posture within seconds. Alone on a mat, that same misalignment can repeat rep after rep — quietly becoming a habit, and sometimes an injury.

That’s the challenge that inspired a mat which could sense what an instructor would normally see. I created FormAlign to bring the precision of expert instruction into the freedom and convenience of training at home.

The first prototype — sensors, a microcontroller, and a lot of iteration — proved the idea works: posture and alignment can be measured directly through the mat, and feedback delivered in real time, fast enough to correct a movement while it’s still happening.

But real-time feedback is only part of the solution. The system also tracks performance over time and turns each session into clear, practical insights. More experienced users might simply want to review key metrics and see how they’re progressing toward a goal, while a beginner might want more guidance, personalised feedback, or workouts tailored to their ability and long-term goals. FormAlign can even support rehabilitation and recovery at home — follow a program from a physiotherapist or personal trainer, then train with guidance and feedback whenever you’re ready.

The aim is to make expert-level form feedback, personalised guidance, and progress tracking accessible to anyone — not just those with access to private instruction. This working prototype is just step one. Our goal is to help people move with greater confidence, precision, and purpose.

— Hasini Samaranayake, Founder
