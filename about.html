About

Expert form feedback shouldn’t require an expert in the room.

Founder photo slot Drop in a real photo of you here — a named, visible human behind the product matters more than polish, especially pre-launch.

Hi, I’m Hasini.

FormAlign started with a simple frustration: practicing pilates at home, there was no way to know whether my weight was where it was supposed to be. In a studio, an instructor spots the drift in seconds. Alone on a mat, the same mistake repeats rep after rep — quietly becoming a habit, and sometimes an injury.

So I built a mat that could feel what an instructor sees. The first prototype — sensors, a microcontroller, and a lot of iteration — proved the idea works: weight distribution can be measured through the mat and corrected in real time, fast enough to fix the rep you’re in.

I’m building FormAlign to make expert-level form feedback available to anyone practicing at home — not just people who can afford private instruction. That’s the mission, and the working prototype on the home page is step one.

— Hasini Samaranayake, Founder
